## 💡 光影氛围词库（专业摄影布光完整版）

> **核心原则**：光影是摄影的灵魂，好的布光能让 6 分画面变成 9 分。
> **使用方法**：每个 prompt 选择 **1 种主光技法 + 1 种氛围修饰**，不要堆砌太多（会互相冲突）。
> **进阶技巧**：主光决定画面骨架，氛围光决定情绪，细节光决定真实感。

### 💡 专业布光技法库（12种经典布光）

#### 1. 发丝光 / 轮廓光（Rim Light / Hair Light / Kicker）
> 光源从背后或侧后方打来，在头发和身体边缘形成金色/银色光晕，勾勒轮廓。

`rim light, hair light, kicker light, edge lighting, backlit hair glow, golden hair rim, silver rim light, halo effect around hair, light catching hair strands, backlight outlining body, light wrapping around curves, glowing hair edges, wispy hair illuminated, light leaking through hair, translucent ear glow from backlight, backlight spray, glowing edges, golden outline on body`

**情色应用**：`backlit nude outline, light wrapping around breasts, rim light on hip curve, golden edge on wet skin, hair catching backlight`

#### 2. 侧逆光（Back Side Light / Edge Light）
> 光源从侧后方 135° 打来，比正逆光更有立体感，一侧轮廓发光，一侧保留阴影层次。

`back side light, 135 degree light, side backlight, edge light from behind, asymmetric rim glow, one side rim one side shadow, diagonal backlight, quarter back light, three-quarter back illumination, side kicker, shoulder edge glow, asymmetric outline light`

**情色应用**：`side backlight on breast curve, asymmetric body rim, one side glowing one shadow, edge light on thigh`

#### 3. 伦勃朗光（Rembrandt Lighting）
> 经典 45° 侧光，在脸颊暗侧形成三角形光区，戏剧感强烈，油画质感。

`Rembrandt lighting, 45 degree side light, triangle of light on cheek, dramatic side lighting, painterly light, Dutch Golden Age lighting, chiaroscuro portrait, shadow triangle under eye, classical portrait light, Caravaggio-style lighting, tenebrism, Baroque lighting, Old Master light`

**情色应用**：`Rembrandt light on face, one breast illuminated one in shadow, dramatic body chiaroscuro, painterly nude, Old Master body study`

#### 4. 蝴蝶光 / 派拉蒙光（Butterfly / Paramount Lighting）
> 光源在正上方 45°，鼻子下方形成蝴蝶形阴影，美化面部，经典好莱坞风格。

`butterfly lighting, Paramount lighting, overhead beauty light, nose shadow under, glamorous studio light, classic Hollywood lighting, beauty portrait light, top-down glamour, shadow under nose bridge, cheekbone highlight, forehead glow, glamour shadow`

**情色应用**：`butterfly light on face, overhead light emphasizing cleavage, glamorous body studio lighting, Hollywood glamour nude`

#### 5. 分割光（Split Lighting）
> 光源从 90° 正侧面打来，脸/身体一半亮一半暗，极致戏剧感。

`split lighting, half face lit, 90 degree side light, extreme side lighting, half shadow portrait, light and dark division, face split by shadow, body half-lit, one side in complete shadow, dramatic left-right contrast, chiaroscuro split`

**情色应用**：`split light on body, one breast in light one in darkness, half-lit nude, shadow dividing body, dramatic body split`

#### 6. 环形光（Loop Lighting）
> 光源在 30-45°，鼻子阴影与脸颊阴影不相连，形成小环形，最自然的肖像光。

`loop lighting, 30 degree light, soft nose shadow, natural portrait light, gentle side light, subtle face shadow, flattering face light, soft cheek shadow, natural dimension light, classic portrait loop`

**情色应用**：`soft loop light on face, gentle body dimension, flattering nude lighting, natural skin dimension`

#### 7. 短照明（Short Lighting）
> 脸转向相机的另一侧打光，让阴影面朝向相机，显脸小。

`short lighting, face turned away from light, shadow side to camera, slimming light, narrow face lighting, shadow toward camera, face narrowing effect`

**情色应用**：`short lighting slimming face, shadow side creating mystery, dramatic body short light`

#### 8. 宽照明（Broad Lighting）
> 脸转向光源一侧，照亮面朝相机的那面脸，显饱满。

`broad lighting, face turned toward light, lit side to camera, full face lighting, bright face toward camera, open face illumination`

**情色应用**：`broad lighting on face, full body illumination, open bright body study`

#### 9. 柔光箱 / 包围柔光（Soft Box / Beauty Dish）
> 大面积柔光源，阴影极少，皮肤质感柔和，商业美妆风。

`soft box lighting, broad light, wrap-around soft light, beauty dish, soft diffused studio light, large source soft light, even illumination, minimal shadows, flattering flat light, luminous skin, angelic glow, beauty editorial light, magazine cover lighting`

**情色应用**：`soft glowing skin, angelic nude lighting, luminous body, even skin illumination, beauty editorial nude`

#### 10. 硬光 / 直射光（Hard Light）
> 小面积光源直射，阴影边缘锐利，高对比度，强调皮肤纹理。

`hard light, direct light, focused beam, sharp shadow, high contrast, bare bulb, harsh sun, spotlight, strong directional, intense shadow edge, crisp shadow, dramatic hard light`

**情色应用**：`hard light emphasizing body texture, harsh shadow on curves, high contrast nude, dramatic skin texture`

#### 11. 底光 / 恐怖光（Under Lighting / Uplighting）
> 光源从下方打来，违反自然光规律，营造不安/神秘/情欲氛围。

`under lighting, uplighting, light from below, chin lit from under, eerie glow, unnatural angle, flashlight from below, candle under face, light rising, under chin shadow, nose shadow upward`

**情色应用**：`under lighting on body, light from below illuminating breasts, eerie sexy glow, candle under chin, flashlight on body`

#### 12. 剪影光（Silhouette Lighting）
> 强烈背光，主体完全变黑，只剩轮廓，极致神秘感。

`silhouette lighting, full silhouette, backlit figure, dark outline, body contour silhouette, window silhouette, doorway figure, standing figure in light, pure silhouette, complete shadow figure`

**情色应用**：`nude silhouette, body contour in backlight, standing figure in doorway light, window light silhouette, breast silhouette outline`

### 📐 光影选择原则

1. **场景决定主光**：室内→窗光/灯光，室外→自然光，夜景→人造光
2. **情绪决定调性**：温馨→暖光/烛光，神秘→暗调/侧光，情欲→暧昧/轮廓光
3. **主体决定技巧**：脸部→蝴蝶光/伦勃朗光，身体轮廓→逆光/发丝光，肌肤质感→侧光/硬光
4. **液体决定反光**：湿润皮肤→必须加 `glistening, specular highlights`，液体→必须有 `catching light, translucent shine`
5. **随机性**：每次从以上技法中随机选择，不要总是用同一种布光
